FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this like nightbot?
    No it is not like Nightbot. Nightbot is one bot that many people make use of. This is a way of creating completely custom bots.

  • Does this need Java?
    Yes this does require Java. Java 8 to be exact.

  • Who are you?
    We are two small programmers who go by NthPortal and The_Lone_Devil<TheLoneDevil>

  • Will this work with other streaming services?
    We have added Beam support since Version 1.1.0! Hitbox is not planned right now, but that may change.

  • Why are there so few FAQs?
    This is because we have not been asked many questions, in fact most of these questions are ones we expect people to ask.
